Game Shelf PWA — Release Notes
================================

v1.11.1 — Heart Fix, Tab Refresh, Clues by Sam Parser
------------------------------------------------------
- Heart favorite toggle no longer triggers game card click (proper event isolation)
- Larger heart tap target with padding for mobile
- Tab switching now refreshes the destination screen (home, games)
- Clues by Sam parser updated: matches #CluesBySam hashtag format
- Parser now captures time and difficulty from share text
- Score display includes time (e.g., "19/20 12m")

v1.11.0 — Dynamic Home Grid & Favorites
------------------------------------------------------
- Home screen dynamically sizes: 1 row (1-3), 2 rows (4-6), compact 3 rows (7-9)
- Favorite games: tap heart to pick top 9 for home when 10+ games on shelf
- External games open in Safari on iOS (preserves logins/subscriptions)
